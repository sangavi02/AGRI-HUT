organic fertilizers

Organic fertilizers are fertilizers derived from animal matter, animal excreta (manure), human excreta, and vegetable matter (e.g. compost and crop residues). Naturally occurring organic fertilizers include animal wastes from meat processing, peat, manure, slurry, and guano


organic fertilizer works

Organic fertilizers usually contain plant nutrients in low concentrations. ... They don't make a crust on the soil as inorganic fertilizers sometimes do. They improve water movement into the soil and, in time, add structure to the soil. Organics feed beneficial microbes, making the soil easier to work


types of organic fertilizers

  • dry organic fertilizers
  • liquid organic fertilizers
  • growth enhancers
